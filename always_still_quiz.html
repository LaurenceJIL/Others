🏢 Office — New employee
The team manager ______ sends a welcome email to new members on their first day.
✅ Always is correct. We use always for a habitual action that happens every time — sending a welcome email is a consistent routine.
2.
💼 Project management
We submitted the proposal two weeks ago, but the client hasn't replied ______.
✅ Still is correct. We use still with a negative to show something hasn't changed when we expected it to. The client's silence continues — it's ongoing and unexpected.

🏥 Healthcare — Admin

✅ Always is correct. Confirming appointments 24 hours before is a regular procedure — it happens every time. That's a perfect use of always.

✅ Still is correct. Still shows that a situation continues longer than expected. The team expected the figures to arrive earlier.
