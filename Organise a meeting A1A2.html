Key Learning Points:

✅ "I'm sorry, I'm busy"
Use this polite phrase when you cannot meet at the suggested time. It's more polite than just saying "No" or "I can't."

✅ "How about..."
Use this to suggest alternative times, places, or activities. It shows you're still interested in meeting and being helpful.

✅ "...is fine with me"
Use this to politely agree to someone's suggestion. It sounds more natural and friendly than just "OK" or "Yes."
💡 Pro Tips:
• Always suggest a specific alternative when declining
• Ask follow-up questions to keep the conversation going
• Be specific with times and places when making suggestions
• Use these expressions in professional and casual situations
