📋 Quick Reference

First Conditional — Real & Possible
If + present simple, will + infinitive
"If the client approves the budget, we will start next week."
Second Conditional — Hypothetical
If + past simple, would + infinitive
"If I had a larger territory, I would double my targets."

Discussion Questions
Read each scenario. Discuss with a partner or write your answer using the conditional form indicated. (4 points)
1st Conditional · Realistic
13
Scenario: Your company is about to send a price proposal to a key client. The client has told you informally that they have a limited budget this quarter, but they genuinely like your product.
If you reduce the price by 10%, what will happen? Will the client sign? What risks do you face?
💬 Start your answer: "If we reduce the price, the client will probably…"
1st Conditional · Realistic
14
Scenario: Your manager has offered you the chance to lead a new client pitch next month. You have never led a pitch before, but you feel ready.
If you accept the opportunity, what will be the potential benefits and challenges for your career?
💬 Start your answer: "If I accept, I will gain experience in…"
2nd Conditional · Hypothetical
15
Scenario: Imagine you are the head of sales for a multinational company. You have an unlimited budget and can hire anyone you want.
How would you build your ideal sales team? What strategy would you use to enter a new market?
💬 Start your answer: "If I were the head of sales, I would first…"
2nd Conditional · Hypothetical
16
Scenario: Imagine your company currently has no international presence. You are asked to consider expansion — but there is no concrete plan yet.
If your company expanded into a new country, which country would you choose and why? What challenges would you face?
💬 Start your answer: "If we expanded internationally, I would choose… because…"

Section C — Discussion Notes (open-ended)

Award 1 point per question if the student uses the correct conditional form. Responses are open — the key is grammatical accuracy and logical development. Below are model sentences for reference.

Q13 (1st)"If we reduce the price, the client will sign the contract more quickly, but we will lose some margin." / "If we offer a discount, will we set a difficult precedent for future negotiations?"
Q14 (1st)"If I accept the opportunity, I will develop my presentation skills." / "If I lead the pitch, the team will see me as a more senior colleague."
Q15 (2nd)"If I were the head of sales, I would hire specialists for each vertical." / "I would invest in CRM training if I had an unlimited budget."
Q16 (2nd)"If we expanded into Germany, we would need to adapt our product for the local market." / "I would choose Japan if we wanted to target premium B2B clients."
