Phrasal Verbs for Business Travel

1. Check in: To register at a hotel or airport.
2. Check out: To pay the bill and leave a hotel.
3. Pick up: To collect someone from a specific place.
4. Drop off: To take someone to a place and leave them there.
5. Set off: To start a journey.
6. Touch down: (Of an aircraft) to land.
7. Take off: (Of an aircraft) to leave the ground.
8. Hold up: To delay someone or something.
9. Get in: To arrive (train, flight, etc.).
10. See off: To go to a place to say goodbye to someone.
11. Look forward to: To feel excited about a future event.
12. Look around: To visit and tour a place.
13. Call off: To cancel an event or meeting.
14. Put off: To postpone something to a later date.
15. Set up: To arrange or organize something (e.g., a booth).
16. Get around: To move from place to place in a city.
17. Show around: To give someone a tour of a building/city.
18. Run into: To meet someone unexpectedly.
19. Deal with: To handle a problem or a guest's request.
20. Fill out: To complete a form with information.
